How to Play

  1. At the center of the Math WebGame page, there's a container consisting of a math problem, a number field in which the player can enter their answer, and a button the player clicks to submit their answer. Also, there's a timer which ends the game when it expires, so they should answer each question as quickly as possible.
  2. The player's score starts at 0 points. If the player answers a question correctly, their score will increase by 10 points. Otherwise, their score will decrease by 5 points. Note that there's a multiplier which increases by 1 if the player answers a question correctly and decreases by 1 otherwise. The multiplier multiplies its number by 5 points and adds the result to the player's score. (For example, if the player's current score is 10 and the multiplier is 4x, then the score will be 50 if they answered the current question correctly.)
